Structure

What lives where

Canvasframework/canvas/canvas.css owns slide structure and protected selectors.
Clientframework/client/ owns navigation, reveals, counters, and export behavior.
Runtimeframework/runtime/ owns preview, validation, assembly, capture, export, and finalize.
Deck Themetheme.css owns local typography, cards, badges, tables, and canvas variables.
Slide Sourceslides/<NNN-id>/ owns per-slide markup, optional slide-local CSS, and local assets.
Outputsoutputs/<slug>/ stores the PDF, screenshots, report, and summary.
The runtime serves `/decks/<slug>/` as the assembled preview so agents can move fast without drifting outside the framework contract.
