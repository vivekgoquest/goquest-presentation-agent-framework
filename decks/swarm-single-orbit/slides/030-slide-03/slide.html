Cargo Path

A container crosses five layers before it leaves the waterfront

1

Berth

The vessel is positioned, secured, and sequenced for crane work.

2

Crane

Ship-to-shore cranes lift boxes according to the stow plan.

3

Yard

Containers are stacked, sorted, powered, or held.

4

Gate

Appointments and documents decide who can exit and when.

5

Inland

Cargo leaves by truck or rail toward warehouses and distribution centers.
