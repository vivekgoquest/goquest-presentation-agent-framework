Rail Interface

Rail extends port reach, but it introduces hard departure windows and less recovery time

Terminal side

Boxes must be grounded, staged, and loaded in the right sequence before the train build starts.

Rail side

Departure windows are less flexible than truck arrivals. Missing a train can push freight into the next cycle.

Leader takeaway

Night planning should treat rail cut-offs as non-negotiable system clocks.
