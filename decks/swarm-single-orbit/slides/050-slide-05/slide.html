Customs Layer

Cargo cannot move just because it is physically ready

Pre-arrival filing

Manifests, carrier messages, and broker entries are submitted before discharge completes.

Selectivity

Some boxes clear automatically; others are held for document review, scan, or inspection.

Release signal

The terminal needs a clean release before the gate can let that container out.

At night, customs does not disappear. It becomes an asynchronous checkpoint that still decides whether cargo moves by dawn.
