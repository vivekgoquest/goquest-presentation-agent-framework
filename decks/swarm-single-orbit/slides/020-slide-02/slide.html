Why operators use the night window

  • Roads are less congested, so truck turns are more predictable.
  • Terminals can recover yard capacity before the daytime surge.
  • Vessel and rail schedules often land outside normal office hours.

What changes after 10 p.m.

The visible city goes quiet, but the freight city gets denser: fewer bystanders, more machinery, tighter handoffs, and less room for timing mistakes.
