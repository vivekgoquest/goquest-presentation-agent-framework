Data Flow

Every physical move depends on a chain of digital permissions

1

Carrier message

Manifest and vessel data arrive before discharge.

2

Customs signal

Release or hold status updates asynchronously.

3

Terminal plan

The TOS assigns moves, stacks, and lift priorities.

4

Gate release

Driver, appointment, and box status are checked.

5

Inland receipt

Warehouse or rail partners confirm the next handoff.

Most night-shift confusion is not missing labor. It is mismatched data: one system says released, another says held, and the physical box waits in the middle.
