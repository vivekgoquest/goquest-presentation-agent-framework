Security

Access control decides who can enter the system after dark and under what conditions

Security tasks that stay active all night

  • Credential checks for drivers, contractors, and late-call labor.
  • Perimeter monitoring and anomaly response.
  • Escorted access for sensitive cargo or inspection zones.
  • Coordination with law enforcement, customs, and terminal operators.

Leadership lens

A fast gate with weak access control creates downstream safety and compliance risk. A slow gate with rigid checks creates queue risk. Night security has to balance both.
