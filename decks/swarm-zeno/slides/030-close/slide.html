Minimum Evidence

The go / no-go packet should fit on one screen

Build evidence

Commit SHA, deployed version, and the exact config or feature-flag state.

User-path evidence

One screenshot or short note that the core journey worked in the target environment.

Ops evidence

The monitoring dashboard, alert channel, and escalation owner are already live.

Rollback evidence

The rollback step is written down, timed, and understood by the release owner.

Ship packet

If a reviewer cannot scan the evidence in under two minutes, the release is not yet ready for a confident decision.

Keep the packet lightweight: links over long prose, owners over vague status, and real stop conditions over “we’ll keep an eye on it.”
