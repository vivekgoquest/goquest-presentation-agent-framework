Decision Frame

Go when the checklist is calm,
hold when the evidence is fuzzy

Go

  • All four gates are green.
  • The owner can state the rollback path from memory.
  • No open question would surprise users within the first hour.

Hold

  • A proof point is missing.
  • The team needs one more smoke test or owner confirmation.
  • The risk is understood but not yet bounded.

Rollback

  • The critical path breaks.
  • Alerts spike without a quick fix.
  • The team cannot explain the current release state clearly.
Lightweight does not mean loose. A short checklist works only when every item has a named owner and a visible proof point.
