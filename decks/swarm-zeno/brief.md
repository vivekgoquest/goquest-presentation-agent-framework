# Swarm Zeno Brief

## Goal

Create a concise internal presentation that gives a small product or engineering team a lightweight release readiness checklist they can use before shipping.

## Audience

Startup operators, product managers, engineering leads, and the release owner for a small software launch.

## Tone

Clear, practical, calm, and action-oriented. The deck should feel like a working checklist, not a strategy lecture.

## Must Include

- A simple 3-4 slide story
- Four lightweight release gates: build, user path, operator readiness, and rollback
- One slide that shows the minimum evidence needed before a go decision
- One compliant deck-shared asset referenced from `decks/swarm-zeno/assets/`

## Optional Inputs

- none yet

## Slide Goals

- Slide 1: Introduce the checklist and what "ready" means.
- Slide 2: Show the four release gates in a scannable format.
- Slide 3: Show the minimum evidence packet before shipping.
- Slide 4: Close with a simple go / hold / rollback decision frame.

## Constraints

- Keep the story simple and technical rather than decorative.
- Stay inside the deck workspace and use only deck-local or slide-local assets.
- Keep the checklist lightweight enough for a 15-minute release review.
