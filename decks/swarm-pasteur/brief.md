# Swarm Pasteur Brief

## Goal

Create a concise 3-slide presentation that explains incident response as a repeatable
three-move operating rhythm: detect, contain, communicate.

## Audience

Non-specialist operators, founders, and cross-functional leads who need a simple
mental model for handling incidents without technical jargon.

## Tone

Calm, operational, clear, and credible.

## Must Include

- the three moves: detect, contain, communicate
- a short explanation of what each move means in practice
- one visual that reinforces the sequence
- a closing reminder that good incident response is disciplined, not dramatic

## Optional Inputs

- none

## Slide Goals

- Slide 1: frame the three-move model in one sentence
- Slide 2: explain each move with a simple visual and concrete actions
- Slide 3: close with a checklist that feels usable in a real incident

## Constraints

- keep the deck to exactly 3 slides
- keep language plain-English and presentation-ready
- use only deck-local styling and compliant asset paths

## Open Questions

- none
