# Swarm Ptolemy Brief

## Goal

Create a concise three-slide presentation that explains how this framework turns
a plain-English request into a finalized review package.

## Audience

Internal users and non-technical operators who want to understand the framework
workflow without reading implementation details.

## Tone

Clear, confident, operational, and easy to follow.

## Must Include

- the agent normalizes the request into `brief.md`
- deck-wide styling lives in `theme.css`
- slide content lives in `slides/<NNN-id>/slide.html`
- preview, check, export, and finalize are part of the proof loop
- finalized outputs include a PDF, screenshots, report, and summary
- the rules and constraints are there to reduce agent drift

## Slide Goals

- Slide 1: explain the end-to-end promise in one headline and one support paragraph.
- Slide 2: show the workspace pipeline and the guardrails that keep the agent on track.
- Slide 3: show the outputs the user receives and why that feels like a real presentation workflow.

## Constraints

- stay inside the assigned deck workspace only
- use only compliant authoring surfaces
- keep the deck to three slides
- no external or cross-workspace assets
- make the story readable in preview and export even without polish
